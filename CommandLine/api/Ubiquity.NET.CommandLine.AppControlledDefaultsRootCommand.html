Remarks

This type is derived from RootCommand and offers no additional behavior beyond the construction. The constructor will adapt the command based on the CmdLineSettings provided. This moves the hard coded defaults into an app controlled domain. The default constructed settings matches the behavior of RootCommand so there's no distinction. This allows an application to explicitly decide the behavior and support of various defaults that could otherwise surprise the author/user. This is especially important when replacing the internal command line handling of a published app or otherwise creating a "drop-in" replacement. In such cases, strict adherence to back-compat is of paramount importance and the addition of default behavior is potentially a breaking change.
